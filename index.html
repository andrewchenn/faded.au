Welcome to Faded barber studio, Chisholm’s flagship barbershop. Run by dedicated owner Shreyash to create a space where your vision becomes reality.

Before we arrived, barbering in the local area was overlooked with poor communication, outdated styles and rushed services. Now, we aim to inspire change and elevate the craft. Engaged with our community through active social media and content creation, Faded Barber Studio is the place where tradition meets innovation.
